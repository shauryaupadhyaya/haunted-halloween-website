Frequently Asked Questions

This event is designed for adults and mature teens (16+). The haunted house contains intense scares, loud noises, strobe lights, and frightening imagery that may not be suitable for young children. Parental discretion is strongly advised.

Costumes are highly encouraged! We recommend wearing comfortable shoes as you'll be walking and dancing. The venue can get warm, so dress accordingly. Please avoid masks that completely cover your face for security purposes.

Yes, tickets will be available at the door for $35 (cash or card accepted). However, we strongly recommend purchasing early bird tickets online for $25 to guarantee entry, as the event may sell out.

Free parking is available in the mansion's lot on a first-come, first-served basis. Additional street parking is available nearby. We also recommend using rideshare services for convenience and safety.

Yes! We'll have a full bar with themed cocktails, beer, and non-alcoholic beverages. Light snacks and Halloween treats will also be available for purchase throughout the night.

All ticket sales are final. However, tickets are transferable - you can give or sell your ticket to someone else if you cannot attend. Please contact us at least 48 hours before the event to update the name on your ticket.
